Terahertz PCs offers technical support including emergency after hours support, weekend and holiday support. When it's difficult to find an available and highly qualified technician to help your company with its computer problems, Terahertz has your company covered. With this option you are simply billed in accordance with our Weekend/Emergency prices on a per incident basis.

Another advantage is that there are no contracts to sign. You simply make a phone call whenever you feel you need a service and an expert technician comes to your location to repair any computer related problem.

About Us

Terahertz PCs is currently a single person operation, run by Colorado School of Mines student Skyler Morris.

Skyler has been providing IT services for the Denver Metro area for over 5 years, and began by working on the computers of friends and family. After a few years, Skyler went on to work for a couple of the largest consumer electronics suppliers in the United States, before deciding to create his own company, and Terahertz PCs was born.

Our focus is creating a lasting relationship, and providing a timely and affordable service to our clients.
